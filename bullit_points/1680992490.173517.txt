TO: Financial Aid Office
FROM: Samantha, high school senior
SUBJECT: Request for Financial Aid

- Introduce self and provide full name, student ID number, and program name
- Explain financial situation and need for assistance
- Request a meeting with a financial aid counselor
- Outline desired outcomes (scholarships, grants, loans, or tuition payment plans)
- Attach FAFSA form, transcripts, and relevant financial documents
- Emphasize willingness to work hard and determination to achieve academic goals
- Thank financial aid office for their time and consideration
- Express gratitude for any assistance provided
- Take notes and research available options during meeting with counselor
- Stay proactive and assertive throughout the financial aid process
- Keep communication lines open with financial aid office for any updates or follow-up questions.
