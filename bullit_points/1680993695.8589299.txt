- TO: All employees at XYZ Corporation
- FROM: John, HR Manager
- SUBJECT: Policy Update Announcement
- Intention: To communicate major policy updates that will impact all employees
- Goals: 
     - Ensure understanding of policy changes
     - Provide explanation of why changes are necessary
     - Outline specific dates for implementation and compliance
     - Offer contact information for further details and questions
- Tone: Professional, clear, informative, and approachable
- Content: 
     - Explanation of policy changes, including procedures and guidelines
     - Empathetic acknowledgement of potential employee concerns
     - Reiteration of importance and benefits of policy changes
     - Detailed instructions for compliance and implementation
     - Contact information for HR if employees have questions or need further details
- Follow-up: Scheduling meetings or Q&A sessions for further explanation if necessary
